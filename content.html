Future Life Savers

“You don’t need to know the whole alphabet of Safety. The A, B, C of it will save you if you follow it: Always Be Careful.”

Goal of our Workshop


The workshop aims to help people provide immediate assistance to those who are injured. The workshop will help them prioritize health and safety, and help in preparing for disasters.



Mision


Our mission is to simply help people increase their knowledge about first aid kits, and increase their capability of bringing immediate care to themselves.

Vision


Our vision is to prepare people in case of emergency, and have them be capable of looking after themselves and helping those in need of help.

Time & Date


The workshop will start from 1:00pm to 4:00pm. Due to the ongoing, Coronavirus outbreak, we decided to perform our workshop via Zoom Meetings to ensure the safety of the representatives and audience who are participating in our workshop.

Response of the Audiene


We want our audience to know what is the importance of learning something about first-aids that can help them during bad situations by being able to provide basic care to them or to someone. We also want our audience to be able to perform different procedures depending on the severity of the patient. An example of this is knowing how to correctly perform CPR or Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation.

About Our Workshop


Our workshop aims to prepare people and teach them the knowledge they need for helping others. It helps them acquire, develop, and enhance different skills such as communication, critical thinking, focus, and many more. It teaches them how to implement these skills. Having these skills could be useful for many things as these skills are applicable to any topic and situation. It helps them acquire the skills that prevent further injuries.

Why join our Workshop?


There are times where no one can help you but yourself, we want to prepare people in case of emergencies so that they wouldn’t have to depend on others for their safety. We believe that everyone is capable of developing such skills that will help them in situations where these skills are needed.

First-Aid?


It refers to the emergency or immediate care you should provide when a person is injured or ill until full medical treatment is available. For minor conditions, first aid care may be enough. For serious problems, first aid care should be continued until more advanced care becomes available.
